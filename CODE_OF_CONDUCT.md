# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1][cc].

In short: be respectful, assume good intent, give constructive feedback, and
keep conversations focused on the project. Harassment of any kind is not
welcome here.

If you witness or experience behaviour that violates this, please contact the
maintainer at **marcel.duetscher@gmail.com**. Reports are handled
confidentially.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant v2.1 is at:
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
